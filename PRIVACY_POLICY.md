# Privacy Policy for Daily Wisdom Calm

**Last Updated: April 7, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Daily Wisdom Calm ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **User Preferences**: Settings such as notification preferences, theme selection, sound preferences, and wisdom source selection
- **Mood Tracking Data**: Your responses to daily questions and the dates when you answer them
- **Streak Data**: Information about your daily usage patterns and streaks

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information**: Basic device information necessary for app functionality (automatically collected by the operating system)
- **Usage Data**: App usage statistics stored locally on your device

### 2.3 Permissions
The App requests the following permissions:
- **Notifications**: To send daily reminders and meditation completion notifications (optional - you can disable this in settings)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:
- Providing the core functionality of the app (mood tracking, meditation features, streak tracking)
- Sending notifications (if enabled)
- Saving your preferences and settings
- Improving the app experience based on your usage patterns

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage Only
- All data is stored locally on your device using secure local storage
- We do not transmit, upload, or share your data with any servers, third parties, or cloud services
- Your data remains on your device and under your control

### 4.2 Data Security
- We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data stored on your device
- Data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption methods
- We cannot access your data as it is stored locally on your device

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or otherwise disclose your personal information to third parties because:
- We do not collect or store data on external servers
- All data remains on your device
- We have no third-party service providers that access your data

## 6. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data, including:
- No analytics services
- No advertising networks
- No social media platforms
- No cloud storage services

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary action.

## 8. Your Rights and Choices

### 8.1 Data Control
Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your data:
- You can delete the app to remove all stored data
- You can reset app settings to clear preferences
- You can disable notifications at any time

### 8.2 Notification Preferences
You can control notification settings within the app:
- Enable/disable daily reminders
- Change reminder times
- Choose notification sounds

## 9. Data Retention

- Data is retained locally on your device for as long as you use the app
- Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data
- There is no remote backup or data retention on our servers
